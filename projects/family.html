Meet the Family

Terra commands the ground beneath his feet. His stone armor grows naturally from the earth, creating powerful shields to protect his family. Calm and steady, he is the unshakable core of the team.

Inferna generates fire hotter than molten lava. She leads with intensity but loves with warmth. Her flames burn away fear and doubt, igniting courage in those around her.

Volt can sprint faster than lightning and fire electric bursts with precision. He is energetic, curious, and always the first to leap into action.

Aqua controls water in all forms — liquid, mist, and ice. Her soothing energy allows her to heal others, bringing peace and balance to the team.
