1. Title and Topic of My Site

Title: JJ’s Island Adventures – Explore Oahu

Topic: A tourism-style website that highlights different places and activities around Oahu.

I chose this topic because I love exploring Oahu and sharing its beauty. This final project lets me combine the skills I learned in HTML and CSS with real island locations, creating a fun and meaningful website.
