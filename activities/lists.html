What are special entity characters.

Codes that you use when you want to display reserved symbols or characters that are not esily typed on a keyboard.

What are phrase tags.

Phrase tags are special tags that give semantic meaning to text. They tell the browser what kind of text is, not just how it looks

What is the difference between OL and UL tags?

The difference is that OL is mainly about the sequence is in order and is important. The UL is when the order doesn't matter.

What is a DL?

A description list is a type of list that pairs terms with their descriptions. It is often used for things such as glossaries, metadata or Q&A sections
